Class Asteroid

This game was coded in Scratch for my summer homework final game project. Use the left and right arrow keys to rotate and space to shoot. The goal of the game is to shoot everyone and get the most points. Different people have different number of lives.

Connecting Points

Click on the green flag to start and restart the game. Click anywhere on the screen to make a point and when you press spacebar, all the points will be connected with a line.
